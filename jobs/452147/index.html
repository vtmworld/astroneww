Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Job description

As a Content Moderator at Yelp, specializing in Spam Comment Removal, you will play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of Yelp user-generated content. Your primary responsibility will be to review, identify, and remove spam comments and other inappropriate content from the platform. Your work will help ensure that Yelp remains a trusted resource for users seeking reliable and accurate information about businesses and services. Weekend and holiday shifts may be required to maintain content quality at all times.  Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:  * Critical Thinking: Ability to think critically and make informed decisions about content.  * Cultural Awareness: Understanding of cultural differences and sensitivities when moderating content from diverse user bases.  * Data Analysis: Basic skills in analyzing trends in spam content and providing feedback for improvements.  * Customer Focus: Commitment to maintaining a positive user experience on the Yelp platform.  Benefits:  * Health & Wellness: Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  * Work-Life Balance: Paid time off, flexible working hours, and remote work options.  * Professional Development: Access to training programs and career development opportunities.  * Company Perks: Employee discounts, wellness programs, and a supportive team environment.  Why Join Yelp?  * Impact: Play a vital role in maintaining the integrity and quality of Yelp content, helping millions of users make informed decisions.  * Innovation: Be part of a forward-thinking team that leverages cutting-edge technology to combat spam and other content-related challenges.  * Growth: Opportunity to develop your skills in content moderation, with potential for career advancement within Yelp.  * Community: Join a diverse and inclusive workplace that values teamwork, collaboration, and the power of community-driven content.  How to Apply:
