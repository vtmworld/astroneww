Netflix Careers Remote Customer Service  Part-Time

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

At Netflix, we're revolutionizing the way the world enjoys entertainment. With a vast library of award-winning TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more, we strive to provide our members with an unparalleled streaming experience. As part of our customer service team, you'll play a crucial role in ensuring our members receive top-notch support and assistance.

Job Description

As a Remote Customer Service Representative at Netflix, you'll be responsible for delivering exceptional service to our members across various channels including email, chat, and phone. This part-time position offers a flexible schedule, allowing you to work from the comfort of your own home.

Responsibilities:

Provide friendly and efficient support to Netflix members via email, chat, and phone.

Assist members with inquiries related to their accounts, billing, streaming options, and technical troubleshooting.

Utilize Netflix's knowledge base and resources to resolve member issues accurately and... efficiently.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to escalate and resolve complex member concerns.

Stay up-to-date on Netflix's latest offerings, features, and policies to provide accurate information to members.

Meet or exceed established performance metrics related to customer satisfaction, response time, and quality of service.

Maintain a positive and professional demeanor in all interactions with members and colleagues.

Adapt quickly to changes in workflows, processes, and tools to improve efficiency and productivity.

Qualifications:

High school diploma or equivalent; some college coursework preferred.

Previous customer service experience in a remote or call center environment preferred.

Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Strong problem-solving abilities with a focus on delivering solutions.

Ability to multitask and prioritize in a fast-paced environment.

Proficiency in using computer applications and navigating multiple software systems simultaneously.

Comfortable working independently with minimal supervision.

Availability to work a flexible schedule, including evenings, weekends, and holidays as needed.

Benefits:

Competitive hourly rate with opportunities for performance-based bonuses.

Flexible part-time schedule with the option to work remotely from anywhere.

Access to Netflix's extensive library of TV shows and movies.

Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.

Supportive team environment with opportunities for growth and advancement.

How to Apply:

If you're passionate about delivering exceptional customer service and thrive in a remote work environment, we'd love to hear from you! Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your relevant experience and why you're interested in joining the Netflix team as a Remote Customer Service Representative
