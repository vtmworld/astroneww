As a Major Deal Pursuit Leader youll drive the sales process and outcomes on a dedicated basis for major managed services and outsourced deals, grow a profitable pipeline, and close major Information and Communications Technology (ICT) deals with a valuation greater than thirty five million dollars (Total Contract Value) through deal... origination, sales negotiations, and closure. Youll establish the deal direction and manage the deliverance of the assigned pursuit roles, achieving order, revenue, deal margin, and cost objectives. Youll also contribute to the firms growth and development through world-class deal creation and management.  Leading engagement planning and budgeting.  Mobilizing and managing deal teams.  Defining deliverable structure and content.  Facilitating buy-in of proposed solutions from top management levels at the client.  Directing on-time, quality delivery of work products.  Managing engagement economics.  Managing engagement risk.  Managing day-to-day interactions with executive clients and sponsors.  Participating in business development and maintaining contact with executive decision-makers at key clients.  Organizing and leading pursuit teams.  Participating and leading aspects of the proposal development process  Contributing to the development of proposal pricing strategies.

Youll need to have:  Bachelors degree or four or more years of work experience.  Six or more years of relevant experience required, demonstrated through one or a combination of work and/or military experience, or specialized training.  Experience in outsourcing, out-tasking, and/or off-shoring negotiations/deals.  Line/matrix management experience with significant (30 or more) multi-function teams e.g. legal, bid management, commercial, operations etc.  Experience in leading and closing at least one complex network integration deal with TCV of twenty-five million dollars or more, involving at least two or more of the following: people/asset transfer, contract innovations, partner services, and multi-country requirements, and/or leading, implementing, and operationally managed similar transactions as the end-user.  Willingness to travel up to 75% or less.  A valid Drivers License.

Even better if you have:  MS degree in Engineering or another technical discipline.  MBA degree.  Six or more years of industry experience in networked IT services/telecoms, having both domestic and international responsibilities.  Six or more years of fifty million dollar annual revenue/cost responsibilities.  Experience and credibility in successfully working with and presenting to executive board level leaders in large, publicly traded organizations.  Experience as lead/second chair business case development and subsequent commercial contracting/negotiating expertise.
