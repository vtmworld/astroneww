About the Role

The Product Owner will lead the development and delivery of solutions for our Vendor Risk Management and Security Operations product modules.

Responsibilities  Serving as a subject matter expert on Integrated Risk Management, and Security Operations product modules.  Partnering with other product owners to define and refine user stories, acceptance criteria, and functional specifications.  Overseeing and prioritize the product backlog to address both current and future needs of customers and stakeholders.  Planning and lead sprint planning, review, and retrospective meetings with agile teams and stakeholders.  Validating product deliverables, providing feedback and guidance to ensure quality and alignment with expectations.  Communicating product vision, roadmap, and progress to senior management, customers, and other stakeholders.  Conducing user research, market analysis, and benchmarking to identify customer needs, pain points, and opportunities.  Monitoring and assess product performance, quality, and customer satisfaction using various metrics and feedback channels.  Identifying and address issues or risks that could impact product delivery, quality, or scope.

Requirements  Have completed a Bachelor's in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Administration, or a related field.  5 years' experience in a role focused on Integrated Risk Management, Vendor Risk Management, and Security Operations.  Certify as a ServiceNow System Administrator (CSA), or Certified ServiceNow Implementation Specialist (CIS).  Experience with the ServiceNow ESG module.  Experience in agile methodologies, Scrum framework, and product lifecycle management.  Experience for developing innovative, user-centric solutions that provide tangible value.
