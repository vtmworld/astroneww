Remote ESOL (English as Second Language) Teachers Hiring | WFH

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Job Overview

We are seeking dedicated and passionate ESOL (English as a Second Language) Teachers to join our dynamic educational team in a remote capacity. This position offers an outstanding opportunity for educators who are committed to transforming lives through effective teaching, fostering a supportive learning atmosphere, and promoting student achievement...

Key Responsibilities  Create and maintain a respectful and engaging classroom setting with established behavioral expectations.  Design and deliver captivating lesson plans, units, and projects aligned with educational objectives.  Articulate lesson aims clearly to enhance student understanding.  Tailor teaching approaches and resources to meet diverse learning preferences and interests.  Inspire students to explore various academic and career opportunities.  Provide individualized support to learners requiring additional assistance.  Regularly assess and track student progress in academic performance.  Conduct meetings with parents to discuss students growth and development.  Cultivate strong relationships with students, parents, colleagues, and supervisors.  Manage classroom behavior with effective strategies.  Ensure a safe and inclusive environment where all students feel valued.  Maintain ongoing communication with parents regarding their child's progress.  Administer assessments to gauge student learning and development.

Required Skills  Exceptional communication skills, both verbal and written.  Proficiency in curriculum development, lesson planning, and instructional delivery.  Ability to adapt methods and materials to diverse learning profiles.  Strong interpersonal skills for building effective relationships with students, parents, and colleagues.

Qualifications  Bachelors degree in Education or a related discipline.  Valid Illinois Professional Educator License (PEL) or an Educator License with Stipulations (ELS), including required endorsements in ELL/ESL or Bilingual Education.  Previous experience in curriculum design, instructional planning, and teaching is preferred.

Career Growth Opportunities

We are committed to the continuous professional development of our educators, providing access to valuable resources and support that facilitate ongoing growth and learning in the field of education.

Compensation And Benefits

Our educators enjoy a competitive compensation package reflective of their experience, along with comprehensive benefits, including health, vision, and dental insurance, as well as a 401K plan if needed. Additionally, our support from an advisory team ensures a successful and rewarding teaching experience.

Join us in our mission to positively impact the educational journey of our students! If you are passionate about teaching and ready to inspire the next generation, we welcome your application.
