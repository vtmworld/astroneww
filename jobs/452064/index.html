Virtual Administrative Assistant - PT Flex Schedule

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

We are seeking a part-time detail-oriented individual to work as part of a team for two executive level principals and assist in the management, maintenance and updating of their contact databases. The ideal candidate will possess a keen eye for detail, strong organizational skills, and the ability to handle confidential information discreetly.

Key Responsibilities...  Maintain and update contact information within Salesforce for two executives.  Add links to LinkedIn profiles and upload contact photos in Salesforce.  Tag and categorize contacts into different groups for targeted communications.  Track and update attendance for various events, including social, philanthropic, and political gatherings.  Assist in generating and refining invite lists for events based on contact interactions and familiarity with the database.  Ensure the accuracy and completeness of contact data.  Work closely with executive assistants to verify and input new contact details.  Provide regular reports on the status and integrity of contact data.  Identify and rectify data inconsistencies and errors.  Collaborate with IT for Salesforce troubleshooting and updates.

Required Skills and Qualifications:  Salesforce certification is highly desirable.  College degree required.  1-2 years of experience in data management or administrative support, preferably with Salesforce.  Proficiency in Salesforce CRM, MS Office, particularly Excel.  Excellent attention to detail, organizational, and time-management skills.  Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.  Ability to responsibly handle confidential information.  Adaptability to work in a dynamic environment.

Preferred Skills:  Experience with Salesforce CRM and understanding its features and capabilities.  Familiarity with data management principles and database systems.  Previous experience in an administrative role supporting executive staff Apply Job!
