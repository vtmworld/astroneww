Work From Home Flight Reservationist

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Job Title: Work From Home Flight Reservationist

Company: Escape Ensemble Consulting

Location: Remote

Job Description: Escape Ensemble Consulting is seeking a detail-oriented and customer-centric individual to join our team as a Work From Home Flight Reservationist. In this role, you will be responsible for handling flight reservations, ensuring smooth bookings, and providing exceptional service to our clients.

Responsibilities:  Flight Booking: Process flight reservations accurately and efficiently, including ticket bookings, seat selections, upgrades, cancellations, and modifications, according to client preferences and specifications.  Booking Coordination: Coordinate bookings with airlines worldwide to secure the best available options for clients, considering factors such as price, schedule, airline preferences, and travel requirements.  Client Communication: Communicate with clients via phone, email, and live chat to assist with flight inquiries, provide travel... recommendations, and address any questions or concerns promptly and professionally.  Price Comparison: Research and compare flight options from different airlines and booking platforms to ensure competitive pricing and value-added services for our clients.  Confirmation and Documentation: Confirm flight reservations with clients and airlines, providing detailed confirmation documents and instructions to ensure a smooth check-in process for clients.  Payment Processing: Process payments for flight reservations, collect deposits, and manage invoicing and billing in accordance with company policies and procedures.  Travel Assistance: Provide clients with essential information about their flights, including departure/arrival times, layovers, baggage allowances, and any additional services or requirements.  Problem Resolution: Handle any issues or concerns related to flight reservations, such as flight delays, cancellations, or changes, ensuring timely resolution and client satisfaction.

Benefits:  Flexible remote work arrangement.  Competitive salary with opportunities for performance-based bonuses.  Comprehensive training and professional development opportunities.  Travel perks and discounts on personal travel bookings.  Supportive and collaborative team environment.

Qualifications:  Previous experience in flight reservations, customer service, or a related field, preferably within the travel industry.  Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to interact with clients and airline representatives professionally and courteously.  Strong organizational skills and attention to detail, with the ability to manage multiple reservations and priorities effectively.  Proficiency in using reservation systems, booking platforms, and Microsoft Office Suite.  Self-motivated and able to work independently in a remote environment.  Knowledge of airline industry regulations, policies, and procedures.  Ability to adapt to changing priorities and work effectively under pressure.
