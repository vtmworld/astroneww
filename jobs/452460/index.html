Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

You'll be responsible for assisting customers with a wide range of inquiries, from product support to order status, and everything in between. If you're a people-person and enjoy... helping others, this role is perfect for you.

Qualifications:  High school diploma or equivalent.  Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.  Strong typing and computer navigation skills.  Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.  Self-discipline and the ability to work independently.  Previous customer service experience is a plus but not required.

Work Environment:

This is a remote, part-time position, allowing you to work from the comfort of your home. You will need a quiet, distraction-free workspace and a reliable internet connection. Training and support will be provided to help you excel in your role Apply Job!
