What will I be doing in this role?

This team member is responsible for the day-to-day operations of PeopleSoft as well as reviewing, analyzing, and evaluating the PeopleSoft business system user needs. Business systems are segregated by line of business: Materials Management, Grant & Fund Accounting, and Finance. You will be responsible for:  Translating business requirements into design documents for coding,  Investigating errors and/or problems and assisting with a solution/fix,  Writing queries and/or reports,  Performing testing, and  Aiding in the development of training materials.

Primary duties include:  Writing detailed description (requirements) of user needs, program functions, and steps required to develop or modify PeopleSoft applications.  Evaluating and analyzing PeopleSoft application fixes.  Developing and implementing test plans in collaboration with the Systems Analysts (including processes, panels, reports.)  Evaluating new PeopleSoft modules and third party software.  Working closely with other PeopleSoft business analysts  Materials Management, Finance and Grant & Fund Accounting.  Provide preliminary user acceptance of LOB modules and coordinate final user acceptance of Line of Business modules.
