Director of Amazon (Amazon Seller & Vendor, Remote)

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Position Summary:

The Director of Amazon will lead a team of 8-12 brand managers and specialists, overseeing the management of 10-15 brands. This position focuses on high-level strategy, people management, and client relationship management. The ideal candidate will possess a strong technical knowledge of Amazon Seller Central and Vendor Central, with a background in consumer packaged goods... (CPG) and experience managing large advertising budgets.

Key Responsibilities:  Lead and manage a team of 8-12 brand managers and specialists.  Oversee the management and growth of 10-15 brands on Amazon.  Develop and implement high-level strategies to drive brand success and market expansion.  Act as a people manager, providing guidance, coaching, and support to team members.  Manage client relationships at an executive level, including contract maintenance and renewal.  Ensure the team meets and exceeds performance goals, fostering a collaborative and growth-oriented culture.  Utilize Amazon Seller Central and Vendor Central to drive brand success.  Manage large advertising budgets (brand, product, display, DSP) up to $1M.  Promote a data-driven approach to account management and strategic decision-making.  Teach and coach team members to enhance their skills and promote career growth.

Qualifications:  Proven experience as a brand manager with a strong track record of success.  In-depth technical knowledge of Amazon Seller Central and Vendor Central.  Strong understanding of CPG and experience managing large ad budgets.  Demonstrated ability to develop and execute high-level strategies.  Exceptional people management skills with the ability to lead, motivate, and develop a team.  Excellent client relationship management skills with the ability to maintain executive-level engagements.
