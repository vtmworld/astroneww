Join a mission-driven company transforming how college students and their families approach off-campus housing. Our innovative co-ownership model allows students and their families to co-own properties, turning rent into assets that appreciate over time. This not only lowers the cost of living but also builds wealth and creates a lasting financial impact.

Role Overview:

We are seeking motivated and passionate college interns to spread the word about our co-ownership service to parents of college students. As an Outreach Ambassador, you'll help families discover a smarter way to invest in their students' housing by turning rent payments into equity.

Responsibilities:  Engage with Parents: Reach out to parents of college students through various channels (social media, email, events, etc.) to introduce them to our co-ownership model.  Educate and Inform: Clearly explain the benefits of co-owning off-campus housing, including financial growth and security.  Build Networks: Connect with other students and their families, creating a community of co-owners who support each other.  Drive Enrollment: Encourage parents to explore our service and help them take the first steps toward co-ownership.  Advocate for Change: Share the positive impact of turning rent into assets and how it can benefit every family.  Collaborate with Research team: Work with research team to refine market research.

What We Offer:  Commission-Based Compensation: Earn commissions for every successful referral that results in a co-ownership agreement.  Real-World Experience: Gain valuable sales, marketing, and communication skills that will enhance your resume.  Flexible Hours: Work on your own schedule, fitting this role around your studies and other commitments.  Meaningful Impact: Help families make smarter financial decisions and support your friends and peers in the process.

Ideal Candidates:  Currently enrolled in college or university.  Excellent communication and networking skills.  Passionate about helping others and making a difference.  Self-motivated, proactive, and able to work independently.  Interest in real estate, finance, or community building is a plus.  Location near campus of universities in:
