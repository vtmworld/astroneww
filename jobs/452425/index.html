The goal of the Field-Based Behavioral Health Care Advocate (Shreveport and Bossier City, LA vicinity) role is to help individuals live their lives to the fullest by promoting recovery and resiliency via coordination and collaboration with multiple internal and external partners including consumers and their families/caregivers, medical, behavioral health, and clinical network teams. Field Care Advocates work with complex and high-risk needs with a goal of engaging the consumer in the treatment process, decreasing their reliance on higher levels of care, helping them to access appropriate community services, and assisting them in improving community tenure.

Field-based Behavioral Health Care Advocate is needed in this area. You will be in the field up to 50% of the time visiting members in their homes, hospitals, and local facilities.

Primary Responsibilities:  Provide case management to members to assess for and find solutions to obstacles to care  Link members to healthcare providers and community supports  Work with members via telephone and face to face contacts to address healthcare needs  Provide field visits to members who have been admitted to behavioral health hospitals  Meet with members in the hospital and their home  Assist inpatient behavioral treatment team with discharge planning  Collaborate treatment plans with outpatient providers  Assist the member with transitioning back into the community following an out of home placement  Work with members to assist with getting their medications filled, making follow up appointments and arranging transportation to medical appointments  Explain behavioral health insurance benefits and what behavioral health services are available

Youll be rewarded and recognized for your performance in an environment that will challenge you and give you clear direction on what it takes to succeed in your role as well as provide development for other roles you may be interested in.
