### **Job Description:**

Cigna is seeking a detail-oriented and proactive Secretary to join our dynamic team in a fully remote capacity. As an organization committed to promoting health and well-being, we are on the lookout for a motivated individual who embodies hard work and a passion for supporting operational excellence. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional organizational skills, a commitment to continuous improvement, and a keen interest in contributing to a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment.

### **Key Responsibilities:**

1. **Administrative Support:** - Provide comprehensive administrative support to the team, including but not limited to scheduling meetings, managing calendars, and organizing documentation. - Assist in preparing reports, correspondence, presentations, and other materials as needed.

2. **Communication:** - Serve as a point of contact between team members and external stakeholders, ensuring effective and professional communication at all times. - Manage incoming and outgoing communications, directing inquiries to the appropriate party while maintaining a high level of confidentiality.

3. **Data Management:** - Maintain and organize electronic filing systems and databases, ensuring data accuracy, consistency, and availability. - Support the team in tracking various metrics and performance indicators.

4. **Meeting Coordination:** - Schedule and coordinate meetings, webinars, and events, including logistics and materials preparation. - Take minutes at meetings and distribute them to relevant stakeholders promptly.

5. **People Management:** - Foster a collaborative and positive atmosphere within the team, helping to promote innovation and problem-solving. - Play an active role in onboarding new team members and facilitating their integration into the team culture.

6. **Project Management:** - Assist with ongoing projects by monitoring deadlines, deliverables, and outcomes while ensuring compliance with company policies and standards. - Collaborate with team members to prioritize tasks and support the successful completion of projects.

7. **Professional Development:** - Stay updated with industry trends, tools, and techniques related to administrative tasks and support. - Participate in training and professional development opportunities provided by Cigna to build upon skills and expertise.

### **Qualifications:**  *Required Skills and Experience:** - High school diploma or equivalent; relevant coursework or certifications in administration or business preferred. - Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) and familiarity with remote communication tools (Zoom, Slack, etc.). - Excellent verbal and written communication skills with a keen attention to detail.  *Personality Traits:** - Hardworking and dedicated with a strong work ethic. - Highly motivated with a proactive approach to problem-solving and task completion.  *Soft Skills:** - Strong people management skills with a focus on teamwork and collaboration. - Innovative mindset with the ability to think critically and creatively to improve processes and outcomes.
