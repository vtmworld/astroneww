Technology Support Technician (SP)

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Job Summary The primary purpose of this role is to provide level 1 and basic level 2 technical support to resolve commonly reported, semi-complex problems with technology products and applications, including computer hardware and software, telecommunications equipment, and networks operating in Lowe's Stores, Supply Chain facilities and corporate office locations. This includes performing... tracking and documentation by entering details of problems, status of service requests, and resolutions into the company incident management system. This role responds to customer requests via phone queue, chat, and customer emails, creates/updates knowledge articles and support processes, and participates in the execution of disaster recovery and business continuity processes, supporting these events as they arise. This role also performs the setup and maintenance of computer systems by installing and/or upgrading hardware and software. This role interacts occasionally with third-party hardware and/or software vendors by initiating and supporting vendor services as necessary. The individual in this role works the hours and schedule required to support the enterprise and may be on call and/or work alternate schedules to meet the needs of the department. Qualifications Minimum Qualifications  Associate's Degree in Computer Science, CIS, or related field (or equivalent work experience in a related field) 2 years IT experience in a support or operations environment working with PC or client/server platforms and/or hardware  2 years of experience requiring the use of a PC or company information system Preferred Qualifications  Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, CIS, or related field 1 year of IT experience in the retail industry Pay Range: $20.28 - $33.81 per hour Starting rate of pay may vary based on factors including, but not limited to, position offered, location, education, training, and/or experience.
