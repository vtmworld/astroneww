The Wave Planner is responsible for allocating orders, releasing work to the warehouse floor, balancing workflow, and ensuring inbound and outbound truck turn times are within acceptable levels to meet customer needs. Assigns and monitors the work including staging, wave times and pick assignments for various teammates. Core Responsibilities Assigns and monitors distribution including staging, wave times and pick assignments for various teammates. Balance drop dead times versus product availability. Ensures proper order allocation by shipment ID or shipment line depending on the load. Prepares trailer loading reports for all outbound loads, organizes/prioritizes the paperwork to each loader as they complete loads. Prioritizes any inbound loads, notifying receivers if necessary. Receives completed paperwork for both inbounds and outbound loads with discrepancies and finishes appointments. Checks for any shorts that can be filled or were cut unnecessarily and any other special requests before closing out the trucks. Determines which doors or yard locations trailers should be positioned. Observes priorities of all work and manages priorities of live loads, to make sure we have prioritization of work and loads in the queue. Responsible for escalating any system problems. Assists co-workers with questions regarding picking issues. The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by people assigned to this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties, and skills required of personnel so classified. May perform other duties as assigned. Ability to be respectful, approachable and team oriented while building strong working relationships and a positive work environment.
