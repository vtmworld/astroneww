WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING: This position will entail working as the Customer Experience Representative in the Claims Department, aka, the hero for our team, who saves the day by making things right by the customer. That means you will handle the damages that occur on moves and work to get them settled by coordinating with the customers to get their damaged item(s) repaired, replaced, or compensated for via a cash settlement. One of our big differentiators is our "No Worries Damage Coverage" (https://www.einsteinmoving.com/why-us/no-worries-damage-coverage/) which allocates up to $2,000 per move to repair, replace, or provide a cash settlement for any item damaged by our movers during the moving process. This will give you wide latitude to find a solution that fits each customer you work with (or at least 99.9% of them). So, when handling damages, you will work with management and individual movers to find out what happened. You will work with vendors and suppliers to find replacement parts and or repair options. And you will work with customers to make sure their move ends on a happy note. To make sure you are successful you will train with the head of the Customer Experience Department, get a fully vetted and up-to-date vendor list, and have access to training materials and example calls. We know that your success is our success so we will make sure you have what you need. Another big piece of this job will include expanding our growing vendor list, building relationships with vendors, recognizing damage trends, and offering ideas or solutions for continuous improvement. Some tasks will be fun. Some will not be so fun. We do promise that working with us won't be boring though.If you think this all sounds like something you would be good at and enjoy, please be sure to include the phrase My attention to detail is no secret in the Do you have previous experience with customer service question on the application.
