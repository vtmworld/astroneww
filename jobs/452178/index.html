THE WORK

This position will support Integrated Program Planning within the IWSS line of business. Provide planning and schedule management and control expertise. Develop and maintain program IMS. Ensure adherence to planning processes, business rhythms, metrics, control and reporting mechanisms. Key interface to program management, subcontractors and the customer for program status. Conduct critical path & risk analyses and output into actionable data for the Program. Communicate schedule impacts and develop corrective action proposals.
