IT - Enterprise Data Platform - Sr Business Systems Analyst

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

JOB TITLE: Senior Business Systems Analyst

DEPARTMENT: Information Technology

STATUS: EXEMPT

JOB CODE: 4509

PAY SCALE: $97,600.00 - $105,000.00 Annually

GENERAL DESCRIPTION:

The Senior Business Systems Analyst is responsible for translating business data needs into data warehousing and reporting solutions through close collaboration with business teams to identify business requirements enabling data driven decision making throughout the enterprise.

TASKS, DUTIES, FUNCTIONS:

1. This role covers all aspects of the Software Development Lifecycle with respect to data warehousing and reporting solutions. Requirements analysis, report content identification and report functionality analysis, SDLC documentation, and other recommend designs/solutions will be necessary to meet business or other requirements.

2. Determine and recommend reporting enhancements or improvements for existing as well as new processes through the use of enterprise business intelligence tools to enhance the... analysis capability or operational efficiency of various business units within the organization. This includes working across business areas, applications, data domains and technologies.

3. Document business requirements (BRDs) and functional design specifications (FRDs) for conversions, interfaces and reports.

4. Drive efficient and effective joint business/IT requirements and solution discussions in addition to stakeholder review meetings.

5. Identify functional gaps and opportunities of improvement.

6. Coordinate and drive issue resolution with cross-functional teams during the design and development process.

7. Work closely with users to define and document use cases, scenarios and test scripts.

8. Prepare and execute test plans to ensure solution/design meet business requirements.

9. Create documentation for processes, training and operational support.

10. Ensure requirements are delivered in timely stages, allowing the design and development to make the necessary progress.

11. Maintain a thorough understanding of state and federal laws and regulations related to credit union compliance including bank secrecy and anti-money laundering laws appropriate to the position.

12. Ensure solutions within the environment follow established guidelines.

13. Research, define and evaluate data requirements and specifications for departmental projects.

14. Perform evaluations of data and data structures from disparate systems. Proactively seek opportunities to understand and improve business processes through data business intelligence and data analytics.

15. Mentors and trains other Business Systems Analyst as needed.

16. Act as liaison between business clients and technical staff throughout the life cycle of projects or process improvements. Coordinate effectively with IT and other departmental projects analysts and managers.

17. Stay current with technology trends, solutions and vendors.

18. Perform other job-related duties as necessary.

PHYSICAL SKILLS, ABILITIES, AND EXERTION UTILIZED IN THE PERFORMANCE OF THESE TASKS:

1. Effective oral and written communication skills required to interpret, resolve, and document networking problems.

2. Occasional local, statewide and inter-state travel may be required.

3. Periodically work extended and weekend hours.

4. Ability to work under pressure.

5. Must possess sufficient manual dexterity to operate standard office equipment such as PC, copy and fax machines and telephones.

ORGANIZATIONAL CONTACTS & RELATIONSHIPS:

1. INTERNAL: All Credit Union Staff

2. EXTERNAL: Vendors
