National Sales Director Food Broker- Costco Wholesale

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

The National Sales Director contributes to the success of The Company by generating new business opportunities, acquiring new customers, and selling additional products with existing vendors in a fast paced environment. This is done in tandem by managing buyer relationships and expectations and working closely as a member of our Sales team. This position requires exercise of discretion in prioritization of work and reports to the Managing Director.

Job Duties and Responsibilities

· Focus sales efforts on identifying new market trends and customer needs while increasing value of current vendors.

· Establish rapport and cultivate relationships with Costco buyers, current vendors, and potential new vendors

· Develop growth strategies focused on both vendor and Costco needs, while working closely with vendor and internal team

· Monitor competition by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, and new products, and ensure our vendors remain appropriately... competitive

· Design and implement annual sales plans to develop business and achieve company growth

· Collaborate with regional and corporate Costco buyers to develop rapport, understand their needs, and position our vendors and company for growth

· Meets with Costco buyers in person, virtually, and on phone, and follows up effectively with internal team as well as with vendors

· Works closely with sales support team to ensure current business is managed and maintained

· Ensures vendors are updated throughout the sales and post sales process

· Communicates in a timely manner as an extension of the vendor and works closely with management to ensure value is created for Costco.

· Assists sales support team in proactively solving allocation and delivery issues

· Collaborates with other departments regarding team meetings, assurance of accurate POs, on time shipping, sample tracking and making sure buying teams are kept informed.

· Demonstrate exceptional verbal and written communication skills.

· Additional responsibilities as assigned by manager.
