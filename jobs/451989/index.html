Whats in Store

We're on the lookout for a Texas Workers Compensation / Non-Subscriber Adjuster. As a Texas Workers Compensation / Non-Subscriber Adjuster you will investigate and manage the Workers' Compensation and Texas Non-Subscriber claims of self-insured political subdivisions and Texas Non-Subscriber employers.  Conduct initial three-point contact claims investigation to determine compensability  Pay benefits in accordance with the WC Statute and/or employers ERISA plan document  Work in conjunction with the client and business partners keeping the informed as claims develop  Maintain contact with the injured employee, medical provider, and employer to facilitate return to work  Monitor ongoing medical reports and treatment plans to manage the claim and set reserves accordingly  Review, approve, and manage medical treatment  Review and approve medical bills for adjudication in the Mitchell bill CEP system  Support claim positions before legal proceedings by producing information, reports, and other documents as supportive evidence

This role is a full-time, home-based position. Occasional travel to the client for quarterly claims review meetings may be required.

Your Expertise, Skills, And Abilities  5 years of experience adjusting Texas Workers Compensation and Non-Subscriber claims  Texas Workers Compensation Adjusters license  Technical Workers Compensation claims and medical terminology knowledge  Excellent verbal and written communication skills  Proficient with the use of computers and Microsoft Office Suite  Exceptional organization and time management skills  Demonstrated ability to gather, analyze, and interpret information

Attendance Requirements
