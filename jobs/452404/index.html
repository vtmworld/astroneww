Position Summary

This individual must hold a technician registration in good standing in the state of Idaho and active national certification from ExCPT or PTCB. Technicians will validate electronic prescriptions in compliance with state and federal laws and will direct prescriptions to the pharmacist or prescriber for clarification. Technicians must be able to work business required overtime when needed and accommodate flexible scheduling. To help support development and maximize workflow technicians will be asked to perform other functions that may include but are not limited to the entry of prescriptions into the computer system, performing calling functions, faxing prescriptions for clarification, substitution and formulary changes, and labor sharing efforts with other CVS units.

Required Qualifications

Must have a an active technician registration with the Idaho Board of Pharmacy.

Must have a an active/valid technician national certification through ExCPT or PTCB.

Must be available for onsite training for a week in December.

Preferred Qualifications

Prefer at least 6 months experience as a pharmacy technician.

Prefer 3 years of experience as a pharmacy technician.
