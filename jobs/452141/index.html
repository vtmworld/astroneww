Position: Executive/Administrative Assistant

The Executive/Administrative Assistant is a crucial member of the Global Treasury team, providing organizational support and ensuring the team's operational needs are met while fostering a positive work environment. If you are a proactive individual with strong organizational and interpersonal skills who thrives in a dynamic, fast-paced environment, we encourage you to apply.

Key Responsibilities:  Provide daily administrative support to three Vice Presidents (VPs), including managing calendars, coordinating travel, and handling expense management.  Support team-related tasks, including onboarding and offboarding, supply management, and ad hoc projects like employee engagement initiatives.  Manage high volumes of incoming and outgoing emails and calls with discretion, particularly when handling sensitive and confidential information.  Coordinate travel and expense management, including preparing necessary travel documents and booking complex domestic and international travel arrangements.  Actively participate in meetings, manage organizational charts, and maintain team distribution lists.  Assist team members with process-related questions and best practices.  Oversee departmental assets, procure supplies and services, and perform other administrative tasks as needed.  Prepare correspondence, conduct research, and organize pre-meeting materials for review.  Perform other related projects and duties as requested.

Minimum Qualifications:  Proven experience as an administrative assistant.  Excellent organizational and prioritization skills with strong attention to detail, especially in calendar management.  Ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently and meet deadlines.  Strong communicator with the ability to influence at all organizational levels.  Demonstrated ability to exercise good judgment, work independently, anticipate needs, and be resourceful.  Positive attitude and energy, valuing personal excellence and a strong work ethic.

Preferred Qualifications:  Highly proficient in Microsoft Outlook; proficiency in Excel, Word, and PowerPoint is a plus.  Knowledge of platforms such as Concur, SharePoint, Cisco/WebEx, Ariba, Pilot, Workday, and TechCare is preferred.

Additional Information:  Candidates must be eligible to work in the U.S., as American Express does not sponsor visas for this position.  Compensation will be determined based on location, experience, and other job-related factors.  We offer a competitive salary, incentives, a 6% company match on retirement savings, free financial coaching, comprehensive health benefits, flexible work arrangements, paid parental leave, access to global on-site health centers, confidential counseling support, and career development opportunities.
