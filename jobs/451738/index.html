Position Overview

We are seeking a dedicated and experienced Mechanic to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in maintaining and repairing a wide range of heavy equipment and vehicles used in demolition and environmental service projects. This role is critical to ensuring that our operations run smoothly and safely, supporting our mission of delivering top-quality services to our clients.
