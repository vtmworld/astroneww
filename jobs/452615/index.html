Job Title: Senior Director of Data Architecture and Engineering...

Job Summary:

Are you a visionary leader in data architecture and engineering? Join Coca-Cola as a Senior Director and take the helm of our data initiatives. This is a unique opportunity to lead the design, development, and implementation of cutting-edge data solutions, all from the comfort of your home.

Key Responsibilities:

Lead and Innovate: Oversee the design and development of data pipelines, solutions, and end-to-end data architecture to support our business needs.

Team Leadership: Manage and inspire a high-performing team of data engineers and architects, ensuring the robustness, security, and scalability of our data systems.

Strategic Execution: Drive the execution of our data product strategy, aligning it with the company's strategic goals and objectives.

MDM and Data Governance: Implement Master Data Management (MDM) and governance practices to ensure data accuracy and consistency across the organization.

Cloud Solutions: Develop and manage cloud-based data solutions, ensuring data protection and regulatory compliance.

Stakeholder Engagement: Build and maintain strong relationships with key stakeholders, understanding their data needs and aligning them with our data strategy.

Advanced Technologies: Utilize big data technologies like Hadoop, Spark, and Kafka, and integrate them with cloud-based advancements to develop state-of-the-art data solutions.

Technical Expertise: Act as a liaison between technical and non-technical teams, translating complex concepts into actionable insights.

Qualifications:

Education: Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, Data Science, or a related field.

Experience: Minimum 12 years in data engineering, with at least 5 years in a leadership role.

Expertise: Proven experience in data strategy, architecture, and MDM implementation.

Technical Skills: Extensive experience with cloud platforms (Azure, Google Cloud, AWS) and big data technologies.

Leadership: Strong leadership abilities with a track record of guiding and motivating high-performing teams.

Communication: Excellent communication skills, capable of articulating complex technical concepts to diverse audiences.

Agile/Scrum: Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies is highly desirable.

Benefits:

Competitive Salary: $35-45K/year, with variations based on location and experience.

Career Growth: Work with an award-winning team at the forefront of innovation.

Comprehensive Benefits: Enjoy a full range of medical, financial, and other benefits tailored to your position.

Flexible Work: Embrace the flexibility of remote work while driving impactful data solutions.

Why Join Us?

At Coca-Cola, you'll be part of a dynamic team dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible. As the Senior Director of Data Architecture and Engineering, you'll have the opportunity to shape our data strategy and contribute to our ongoing success.
