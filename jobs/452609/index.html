Job Description:

The Contractor Consultants is seeking a highly motivated and organized Hiring Specialist to join our dynamic team! As a Remote Recruiter, you will play a crucial role in our recruitment process, ensuring that we find top-tier talent to our clients in the Construction Industry! This is a remote position, allowing you to work from the comfort of your own space.

Prior experience recruiting for (or working in) the Construction Industry REQUIRED!

Responsibilities:  Screen and evaluate applications and resumes to identify potential candidates who meet the required qualifications and skills for various positions at various companies.  Conduct initial interviews with candidates to assess their suitability for specific roles, and provide feedback to the hiring team.  Lead client meetings to ensure company satisfaction with our services.  Coordinate with clients to schedule and organize final interviews, ensuring a smooth and efficient recruitment process.  Obtain feedback from clients after final interviews are completed, enabling us to continuously improve our selection process and facilitate onboarding.  Utilize and maintain various HR software platforms to manage candidate data, interview schedules, and other recruitment-related information.  Collaborate with the HR team to develop and implement effective recruitment strategies and techniques to attract high-quality candidates.

Qualifications:  1-2 years of recruiting experience.  Proven experience in recruitment or talent acquisition within the Construction Industry.  Knowledgeable about construction industry.  Excellent proficiency in the English language, both written and oral, to effectively communicate with candidates and team members.  Exceptional organizational skills with the ability to maintain detailed and accurate records throughout the entire recruiting life cycle.  Strong self-starter with the ability to work independently and take ownership of tasks and responsibilities.  A team player who can collaborate effectively with colleagues and hiring managers.  Motivated and passionate about contributing to the growth of a startup company.  Comfortable working remotely and able to manage time efficiently to meet deadlines.  Bilingual in English and Spanish is highly preferred

Pay:

You will be compensated with a competitive hourly rate ranging from $17 to $19 per hour, commensurate with your qualifications and experience.

Location:

This is a fully remote position, allowing you to work from any location that best suits your needs.
