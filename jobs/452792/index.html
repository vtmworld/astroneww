If you're looking to learn marketing skills quickly in a fast-paced startup environment, work flexible hours, build out your resume, and be responsible for far more than your typical internship, this role could be a great fit.

About the role...

We need someone who can learn quickly and wants to take on more responsibility as they learn. We have a number of different areas where we need additional support. About us

We provide FINRA SIE exam prep, a GRE prep course, a USMLE Step 1 prep course, and ACT prep. Our long-term mission is to help people unlock opportunity in their lives more broadly, guiding them through their life-long journey of education, employment, and career advancement.

We're a small team of five with tons to do and big improvements to be made. You'll be a founding member of our team and one of our first core marketing hires. Although this is an unpaid role to start, we're hoping it will be a great fit that turns into a long-term relationship with the opportunity for growth and a paid position.

We're an entirely remote company. We're comfortable working with people internationally. As long as we communicate well, keep a high quality level, and achieve our deadlines, you can do the work anywhere and anytime you'd like.
