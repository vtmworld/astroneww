Remote Healthcare Manager - Technology | WFH

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Job Overview

Join our innovative team as a Healthcare Technology Manager, where you will be instrumental in transforming the technology landscape within the healthcare sector. Your primary responsibility will be to facilitate the seamless integration of cutting-edge technology solutions while ensuring adherence to regulatory standards. You will lead a skilled team of IT professionals... collaborating closely with healthcare providers to elevate patient care and improve operational efficiency.

Key Responsibilities  Formulate and execute technology strategies that are in harmony with healthcare operations.  Oversee the upkeep and performance of IT infrastructure and systems.  Guarantee compliance with healthcare regulations while safeguarding data security.  Direct technology initiatives, efficiently managing resources to achieve objectives.  Work in partnership with healthcare providers to evaluate and fulfill technology requirements.  Maximize the effectiveness of electronic health records (EHR) and other healthcare software.  Provide comprehensive technical support and training to healthcare personnel to optimize technology use.  Implement and sustain telemedicine and remote monitoring solutions to enhance patient engagement.  Assess and advocate for new technologies to bolster ongoing healthcare projects.  Spearhead disaster recovery planning and establish resilient data backup protocols.  Manage technology budgets and cultivate robust vendor partnerships.  Stay abreast of industry developments and emerging technologies to foster innovation.  Ensure the continuous availability and reliability of healthcare technology systems.  Mentor and nurture a team of IT professionals, promoting a collaborative atmosphere.  Develop and enforce technology policies and procedures to sustain operational excellence.

Required Qualifications  Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Healthcare Management, or a related discipline.  At least 5 years of experience in healthcare technology management.  In-depth understanding of healthcare operations and regulatory standards.  Proven track record in project management and process enhancement strategies.  Expertise in IT infrastructure planning and execution.  Familiarity with electronic health records (EHR) systems and other healthcare technologies.  Strong grasp of data privacy and security regulations.  Exceptional communication and interpersonal abilities, enabling engagement with diverse stakeholders.  Demonstrated leadership skills with a capacity to inspire and motivate a team.  Project management certification is advantageous.  Experience with telemedicine and remote monitoring solutions.  Capacity for analyzing complex issues and suggesting creative solutions.  Awareness of healthcare industry trends and advancements in technology.  Proficiency in budget management and vendor negotiation.  Outstanding organizational skills with the ability to prioritize and manage various projects effectively.

Career Growth Opportunities

This position offers substantial potential for professional development within the dynamic healthcare technology landscape. You will have access to a comprehensive network of experts and leaders in the field, enabling you to enhance your skills and advance your career.
