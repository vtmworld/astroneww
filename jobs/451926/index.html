Summary:

Product Designer Senior

The Product Designer Senior is responsible for bringing innovative ideas, new solutions, and strategic vision to life through outstanding design-no matter what the medium, but especially visual design. The mission of the Innovation Incubator at Dataworks is to find the next big idea in digital innovation. This team is highly collaborative, creative, and scrappy. As a member of the Incubator team, you'll explore problem and opportunity spaces in new technologies, new products/services, and new ways of working. This role is critical to bringing ideas and solutions to life with great design. Under minimal supervision employs customer-centered design principles, skills, methods, and technologies and knowledge of business/product design and development as part of Dataworks Product Design team and capability projects.

Product Designer Lead

Employs advanced expertise of all customer-centered design principles, skills, methods, and technologies and deep expertise of business/product development to lead design project teams through full design activities and all aspects of design projects. Primarily interacts with professionals and management of internal clients. Leads collaboration with Design Researchers, Creative Designers, and interactions with external customers. Performs activities/roles such as: Frames and reframes business challenges to focus design efforts; Empathizes with customers to discover pain points and unmet needs- Synthesizes customer and other research to develop key insights; Brainstorms ideas and develops high impact concepts and business opportunities; Prototypes and tests concepts with customers; Uses effective storytelling methods to convey knowledge or pitch opportunities; Facilitates collaborative design activities and methods; Coaches less senior staff and other employees in customer-centered design principles, skills, methods, and technologies.

Job Description / Responsibilities:

Product Designer Senior

· Leads conversations based on organizational strategy and principles to create alignment.

· Facilitates discussions across teams, ensuring that everyone has an opportunity to share their opinion and be heard, and that discussion outcomes tie to stated goals.

· Guides discussions toward decisions, clarifies user experience strategy, and gets buy-in.

· Works collaboratively with stakeholders to sketch potential use cases or user flows.

· Creates highly detailed design artifacts (flows, journey maps, service blueprints, wires, mocks, prototypes, specs, related documentation) to clearly communicate a compelling desired future state for the product.

· Develops and maintains UI components, patterns, libraries, and guidelines for a cohesive user experience as part of Design System development.

· Organizes content and navigation effectively on an application level (e.g., sitemap) and screen level (e.g., wireframe). Plans and executes card sorting activities with appropriate stakeholders.

· Works with a Lead UX Researcher to select the appropriate research techniques.
