The opportunity as Reporting Analyst

Fella Health is building a world-class team.

As the Reporting Analyst at Fella Health, you'll work closely with our operations team and other key stakeholders to deliver accurate, insightful data analysis and reporting that drives informed decision-making across the organization.

You'll take ownership of developing and maintaining a robust reporting framework, ensuring all teams have access to the data they need to drive success. Your primary focus will be supporting the operations team by analyzing patient data, tracking medication delivery times, and monitoring other key performance indicators. You'll also provide data support to other teams, such as growth, by working with data from ad platforms and sales.

As our data needs evolve, you'll continuously assess our data infrastructure and reporting processes, recommending and implementing enhancements that improve data accuracy, reliability, and accessibility. You'll stay up-to-date with the latest trends in data analysis and proactively identify opportunities to leverage new tools and technologies to drive business value.

Throughout your work, you'll maintain a proactive, solution-oriented approach, leveraging your expertise in big data, data warehouses, and scripting languages like SQL and Python to develop innovative solutions to complex data challenges. You'll be a trusted partner and advisor to teams across the organization, providing guidance and support to help them make data-driven decisions that drive success.

This is a full-time remote role.

You'll report directly to our Head of Ops, as well as work closely with our CEO (Richie Cartwright).
