Do you love the Mac, iPhone, and iPad?Do you love helping people with their technology?Do you want to be more than a Geek, more than a Genius? familiarity with Apple products, especially Mac and iPhone some experience in supporting desktop applications (Office, Creative Cloud, etc.) some experience managing Windows devices in Microsoft 365 (Intune, Entra) experience troubleshooting computer hardware/software and basic networks excellent problem solving abilities

a self-starter and independent worker organized and attentive to detail customer service oriented personable and professional committed to your co-workers and the success of the team ensuring that technology just works for our clients

Compensation is negotiable, depending on your skill level and availability. Part-time and full-time positions available. Daytime availability somewhere between 7am to 7pm Central time M-F is mandatory. Health benefits are also available as part of a compensation package. Maybe the most important benefit is that we are an established, 24-year-old small business, founder-led, with no corporate politics. We value work-life harmony (not just balance) and we always put family first. We've had multiple employees tell us this was one of, if not their favorite job in their careers so far.
