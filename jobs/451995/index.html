(Remote)-Data Entry Specialist at American Express

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Job Overview: American Express is seeking detail-oriented individuals to join our team as Remote Data Entry Specialists. This exciting opportunity allows you to work from the comfort of your home while contributing to one of the most reputable financial services companies in the world. As a Data Entry Specialist, you will play a critical role in ensuring accurate data management and support the overall efficiency of our operations. This position is ideal for those who thrive in a remote environment and are committed to delivering high-quality results.

Key Responsibilities:  Input and manage data across various platforms, ensuring all entries are accurate and timely.  Review and verify the accuracy of information before it is entered into the system to prevent discrepancies.  Maintain and update databases, ensuring information is current and well-organized.  Generate reports based on data entry and assist in compiling data for management review.  Collaborate with team members and... other departments to gather necessary information for data entry tasks.  Identify and resolve data-related issues proactively, ensuring high standards of data integrity.  Follow established guidelines and protocols to adhere to data privacy and compliance standards.  Participate in training sessions to improve skills and stay updated on best practices for data management.

Requirements:  High school diploma or equivalent; relevant coursework or certifications in data management or administration is a plus.  Previous experience in data entry, administrative support, or similar roles is preferred but not required.  Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (especially Excel) and familiarity with database management software.  Strong attention to detail and ability to maintain accuracy while working under tight deadlines.  Excellent organizational skills with the ability to prioritize tasks effectively.  Strong communication skills to collaborate with team members and assist with any data inquiries.  A reliable internet connection and a suitable computer setup for remote work.

Benefits:  Competitive hourly wage, commensurate with experience and skills.  Comprehensive health benefits, including medical, dental, and vision coverage.  Flexible work hours that allow for a balance between personal and professional life.  Paid time off (PTO) for vacations, holidays, and personal days.  Opportunities for professional development and career advancement within the company.  A supportive and collaborative team culture that values employee contributions.
