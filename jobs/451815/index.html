Remote Survey Research Interviewers (Part-Time)

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

OVERVIEW

Hendall is hiring flexible part-time Survey Research Interviewers! We are looking for staff to help conduct structured calls and complete short surveys entering participant responses into a computer assisted telephone interviewing (CATI) system. Calls are made in English only. The pay rate will be $15-$17.75 per hour. Paid training is provided...

DUTIES  Conduct outbound calls structured protocol.  Accurately capture details of each call made into a CATI system.  Able to be on the phone for a full shift and be comfortable in making a high volume of calls with specific time constraints  Complete all training activities  Follow written communication scripts  Maintain standards for quality and production  Be receptive and quick to implement constructive feedback

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS  Candidates must have a bachelor's degree or at least 4 years' work experience to be considered  Demonstrated verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills  Demonstrated active listening skills and the ability to navigate the survey system while accurately entering data  Clear and professional telephone speaking voice and ability to read accurately from a script  Computer literacy skills including keyboard skills, ability to enter data accurately, and basic troubleshooting for using and accessing computer systems  Fluent in reading and writing in English  Ability to work set schedule to meet project objectives which includes a minimum of 20 hours during the calling effort  Hours available are Monday through Friday 12pm to 8pm Eastern.  A set daily schedule of the same work schedule is preferred.  Ability to read scripted questions and responses verbatim  Prior experience as a survey research interviewer strongly preferred  Previous experience collecting and recording data over the phone or in person is preferred  Experience working with MS Office products, such as MS Teams, Word, and Excel
