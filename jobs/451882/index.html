Recruiter (High Volume) - Remote

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Started in 2016, FreedomCare is a Healthcare company that is innovating and revolutionizing the way home care is delivered. We bring dignity and support to patients needing care in their homes and to the caregivers who care for them. We're the largest consumer-directed home care agency in NY State, and are continually expanding our areas of focus including operations in other states and... tech-based innovations.

We pride ourselves on our values which drive the level of care that we deliver to our patients:  Here For You (An attitude of service, empathy, and availability)  Own It (Drive and ownership)  Do the Right Thing (High integrity)  Be Positive (Great attitude and a can-do positive approach to challenges)

Join our team and make a positive impact on the lives of others! We are looking for a High-Volume Recruiter for our Talent Acquisition team.

This is a hybrid or remote position; applicants located in the greater NYC area will be expected to commute into our Lake Success, NY office one (1) day per week.

Department & Position Overview:

As FreedomCare continues to grow significantly and expand our team, our Talent Acquisition team is looking for a dynamic Recruiter. We're looking for someone who embodies an entrepreneurial spirit and thrives in a fast-paced ever-changing environment.

As a FreedomCare Recruiter, you will have a pivotal role in our expansion by leveraging your networking skills and proactive approach to talent acquisition.

Every Day You Will:  Take ownership of the full recruitment lifecycle, from sourcing and screening to interviewing, offer processing, and closing, ensuring seamless experiences for candidates and hiring managers  Proactively source and attract passive candidates; build a robust pipeline of talent for hard-to-fill and high priority positions, while also driving employee referrals  Develop strong relationships with hiring managers and key stakeholders, aligning on role requirements to develop effective sourcing and recruiting strategies, while staying abreast of current and future hiring needs  Keep your finger on the pulse by maintaining an understanding of the market data and creatively identifying top talent  Support talent acquisition process improvement by identifying system and workflow areas of opportunity

Ideal Candidate Will Possess:  3-5 years of demonstrated success in full-cycle recruitment within a high-growth environment  3 + years of proven experience with high-volume recruiting  Exceptional relationship-building skills with a focus on client services and clear communication  Solution-centric, with an entrepreneurial mindset coupled with a creative approach to problem-solving.  Strong analytical abilities, leveraging data to inform recruitment strategies  Exceptional organizational and multitasking abilities, with willingness to roll up your sleeves and contribute to our collective success  Thrives in a fast paced, dynamic work environment  Experience using an ATS  Experience using LinkedIn Recruiter Lite/Recruiter

Nice-to-Haves:  Experience recruiting within a healthcare, startup or high growth environment  Previous agency recruiting experience  Experience using Greenhouse
