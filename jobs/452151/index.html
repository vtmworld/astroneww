Executive Inquiry Leader, Executive Inquiry | Amazon

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

The Executive Inquiry Leader (EIL) is responsible for maintaining and improving the health of executive summary requests within Customer Service. They accomplish this by creating streamlined executive level summaries, assisting stakeholders with executive requests, problem solving unique customer issues, influencing on a global level and excelling in a fast paced environment.

The EIL will have effective verbal and written communication skills as the position requires communication to senior stakeholders including Directors, VPs, Legal, Public Relations, etc. In addition to executing summary requests, the EIL will ideate and deliver projects to improve the Exectutive Inquiry program and the overall customer and stakeholder experience. An ideal candidate will have experience in driving adoption and inspecting implementation to ensure all solutions scale and go beyond good intention processes.

Key job responsibilities  Identify root cause issues of customer escalations and drive... stakeholders to fix those root cause issues with efficient and scalable mechanisms.  Identify, own, and manage medium to high complexity cross-departmental initiatives and projects.  Write executive-level summaries for C-level and Director audiences.  Seamlessly adjust language and communication methods for tech and non-tech audiences, as well as those deeply familiar with process and those new to process.  Develop expertise in internal systems, departments, policies, and procedures.  Create and continuously improve strategies and procedures.  Achieve established targets and goals for service level agreements, quality expectations and project deliverables.  Actively follow up on action items stemming from executive summaries, measuring the effectiveness of the implemented solution to ensure the customer experience is improved.

A day in the life You will resolve the most highly escalated customer contacts received by Amazon by working closely with stakeholders and senior leadership across the company to research unique customer issues, identify root causes, and create action items to address and fix root causes. You will achieve this through scrupulous deep dives, excellent communication and stakeholder management, and strong time management. Between owning executive summary requests, you will drive projects to improve the efficacy and impact of the Executive Inquiry team.

Basic Qualifications

 Bachelors Degree or 2+ years of equivalent experience  2+ years in a Customer Service role  2+ years project management experience  2+ years in an Executive Escalations role or role with experience handling high visibility, escalated scenarios requiring excellent written and verbal communication

Preferred Qualifications

 2+ years experience in Amazon D2AS  Excellent judgment, discretion, composure, and professional attitude  Ability to influence others without having positional authority  Global Program and Project Management proficiency  Experience in process improvement methodologies  Demonstrated ability to make courageous recommendations and decisions to drive significant organizational change  Passion for ensuring a world-class customer experience with every contact  Experience working virtually
