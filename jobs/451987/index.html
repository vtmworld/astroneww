Data Analyst Supply Chain (Remote)

We are looking for a Data Analyst for our client, a leading global security and aerospace company. In this role, you will decompose existing data modeling processes and procedures. Document processes and procedures to construct data model attributes & attributes. Construct sections of a narrative document instructing various user groups how to perform... operations to reconstruct data model attributes & attributes.

This is a 13-month contract (extensions likely), 40-hour-per-week (4-day 10-hour shifts per week) role, this is a remote role in the US.

This is a W-2 role as a Stage 4 Solutions employee. Health benefits and 401K are offered.

Responsibilities:  Develops, inspects, mines, transforms, and models data to raise productivity, improve decision-making, and gain competitive advantage in an engineering setting.  Document processes and procedures to construct data model attributes & attributes. Conducts statistical analysis of data and information to ensure correct predictive forecasting or classification.  Manages all aspects of end-to-end data processing utilizing customized report-building functions of systems. Maintains analytical systems, verifies the accuracy of the data, and acts as liaison with business.  Works with technical developers and data scientists to define business outcomes and govern capabilities.  A focus on supply chain design and execution is necessary to mitigate the following: program delays, impaired customer/partner relations, and inefficient Company operations.  Execute and coordinate the onboarding of the suppliers into the Supplier Network Collaboration (SNC) and Transportation Management System (TMS).  The candidate will work within the defined process to ensure suppliers are meeting the time-sensitive goals aligned with the system deployment schedule.  The position includes frequent supplier interactions including communicating the strategy and the tasks associated with SNC/TMS acceptance.  Daily troubleshooting and supplier assistance related to SNC/TMS questions, onboarding efforts and issues.  Assist with status reports for leadership reviews. Assist in developing mechanisms for monitoring project progress and for intervention and problem-solving with the project manager, suppliers and internal customers.  Manage and track supplier task completion, provide system access based on training completion, and support team efforts to ensure security levels are completed and accurate.  The most desired skill sets include the ability to collaborate cross-functionally, build consensus, shape/influence opinion and articulate the future state.

Requirements:  5+ years of experience manipulating large data sets.  Experience in Python/Pandas.  Experience in SQL.  Bachelor's Degree.
