Dispatch Coordinator Tue-Sat 2nd Shift

The Dispatch Coordinator is responsible for the effective management and coordination of a fleet of drivers and vehicles for multiple locations. The Dispatch Coordinator will work independently, serving as the first point-of-contact for drivers to rely on regarding deliveries and pickups. One of the most fundamental aspects in this role will be helping the operations run efficiently, assist with day-to-day needs, and essentially, having the flexibility to multi-task and complete assignments timely. A great opportunity for a dedicated, and reliable individual.

What You Will Do  Communicate with operations daily to assess dispatching needs.  Schedule and dispatch drivers and agents to appropriate locations according to customer requests.  Act as liaison between drivers and customer to effectively complete service.  Thorough record-keeping, periodical route surveillance, and time logs reviews to be performed daily.  Coordinate vehicle maintenances such as repairs, washes, and substitutes.  Report operation status and flagged issues periodically to management.  Other duties as assigned.

What You Bring  At least 1 year of customer service experience  Must be at least 18 years old and have a high school diploma or GED  Be proficient in: Microsoft-Word, Excel, Outlook & data entry and type 30 WPM  Knowledge of US and world geography and time zones is preferred  Call Center experience is preferred  Must have outstanding communication, customer service, and organizational skills  Be able to operate in a fast-paced and demanding environment  Be flexible - with the ability to work additional hours as business requires
