Key job responsibilities

Associate Technical Account Manager

Associate Technical Account Managers (TAM) support AWS customers' creative and transformative spirit of innovation across all technologies, including but not limited to: Compute, Storage, Database, Big Data, Application-level Services, Networking, Serverless, Deployment, and Security. Associate TAMs provide advocacy and strategic technical guidance to help plan and build solutions using best practices, and proactively keep customers' AWS environments operationally healthy.

Associate TAMs work with both technical and business leaders at AWS customers. Longterm planning and driving toward successful technical and business outcomes is an Associate TAM's primary focus. Associate TAMs do this by leading both tactical and strategic activities for AWS customers such as issue escalation, launch support planning, training initiatives, operational reviews, cost optimization, and more. Associate TAMs advocate for their customers with AWS leadership at all levels.

Internally, Associate TAMs are able to dive deep and contribute to the larger AWS community. Associate TAMs are able to do this via Technical Field Communities; Areas of Depth; content development; leading or contributing to projects; and many other activities.
