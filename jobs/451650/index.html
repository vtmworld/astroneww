Employee communications specialist  Employee Engagement Communications The Employee Communications Specialist will help create comprehensive, engaging communications and events that drive engagement, connection, and a sense of understanding and pride in F5s culture, business, and impact on the world. The successful candidate is both creative and structured in their approach and has a proven ability to plan, create, and launch engaging content for high visibility employee communications and events. Excellent written and verbal communication skills, a focus on teamwork and collaboration, and a global viewpoint are essential. A successful candidate brings a creative approach that puts F5 employees at the center of the experience. Excellent attention to detail and embrace and understand various viewpoints to support F5s diverse, growing, global business are essential. This role is based in Seattle, WA, and is expected to participate in our office-based culture. Primary Responsibilities: Works with the senior director of the Employee and Executive Communications Team to create and manage engaging employee events that support F5s business and culture including F5s quarterly company employee meeting, manager meetings, and others along with connected programs across digital and traditional channels. Create, launch, and measure communications to help illustrate value and gain data for continual improvement. Establish globally repeatable processes, templates, and best practices around employee events to create a consistent experience for all employees. Collaborate with employees and leaders across the company to ensure events and communications are inclusive of F5s diverse employee base. Consult with leaders and teams to position and align communications efforts. Effectively use data to measure program impacts and inform future work. Other Responsibilities: Writing, editing, project management, and other duties as assigned. Able to create engaging PowerPoint slides and presentations and video content is a definite plus! Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Experience creating, managing, and measuring high impact employee events and communications. Experience in working and collaborating with international colleagues to represent our global culture. Creative ideation, writing and editing skills, development of original content and the ability to construct well-written stories, PowerPoint presentations, and short video content. Strong project management and execution experience, including measurement. Creative doer balanced with an appreciation for data and metrics.
