Customer Education Consultant - per diem - remote

Posted on 11/5/2024 Part Time Remote - US

Company Description

Renaissance is a global leader in assessment, reading, and math solutions for pre-K12 schools and districts, committed to providing educators with insights and resources to accelerate growth. Renaissance offers a rich multi-layered instructional ecosystem combining workflow software, assessments, analytics, and instructional solutions on an open and integrated platform that allows educators to focus on improving individual student performance at scale. Serving more than 40% of schools in the U.S. and more than 16 million students across the globe, Renaissance is one of the most recognized and trusted brands in Pre-K-12 education.

Job Description

Renaissance Learning, Inc. is seeking Customer Education Consultants with a background in education and strong facilitation skills to deliver high-impact, engaging professional learning for Renaissance users. Working closely with Customer Training Managers and the Director of Professional Learning, Renaissance... Consultants help educators apply learning to practices for lasting growth. Renaissance Consultants are key members of our Customer Education team and are responsible for facilitating top quality training experiences, building positive relationships, supporting educators in assessment and data practices, and contributing to the continuous development of Renaissance professional learning programs.

Renaissance Consultants are scheduled on an as-needed basis and paid a daily rate with reimbursements for travel and related expenses. All Consultants must be available for onboarding in late May and training in June. Consultants must have a flexible schedule to accommodate a minimum of five training days per month and additional days during peak summer and fall months.

In this role, you will:  Learn and become an expert in Renaissance solutions.  Customize and deliver on-site and/or virtual product training and professional learning that meets client needs and exceeds expectations.  Differentiate training on the spot when needed to ensure high engagement and meeting learning goals.  Engage in an internal cycle of reflection, feedback, and development (including observations, client evaluations, and coaching)  Prepare for training engagements by engaging with Renaissance team members, customizing training materials (within specific parameters), learning about the particular needs and goals of the client, etc.  Document and submit detailed training notes.  Present a professional image to clients at all times.  NOTE: We are ideally looking for candidates who do not want to supplement this role with a full-time role or another part-time position. This role works best for retired educators or for people who are not currently or planning to work full-time.
