Licensed Therapist We are regularly hiring licensed therapists for 100% teletherapy positions nationwide. As a leader in telepsychiatry, we are continuously expanding our clinical entity network along with our clinician network, and strive to match clinicians to opportunities as quickly and as often as possible. Having your current information on hand means you will be one of the first... people we reach out to when a potential match becomes available. To be sure we have all of the information we need, please click "apply" to submit your contact information and resume/CV. Please note that this form is not an application for a specific position opening, but rather a way to express interest in any current and future openings that may be a match.  About Array With over 20 years of experience with telepsychiatry, Array provides operational, administrative and technical support so our behavioral care clinicians can focus on clinical care. We work with our telepsychiatry clinicians to understand their schedule and preferences and then match them to employment opportunities that will be a good fit. Array has clinicians who work on a full-time and part-time basis. To learn more, visit arraybc.com. All roles:  Provide services to individuals who otherwise may not have access to care  Work from home: No traveling or commuting to work  Excellent clinical, operational, administrative, and technical support  Rapidly-growing, connected community of Array Clinician peers  Medical liability coverage provided  As needed, funding for and administration of licenses beyond your home state  Training in delivery of high-quality telebehavioral health

What Our Clinicians Love About Array Clinical Integration:  Our psychiatrists and therapists greatly value that Array empowers them to practice as similarly as possible to how they would if they were physically present, ensuring continuity of care, care team integration, EMR access, and in-person support. Serve the Underserved:  Array's mission is to enhance access to high-quality mental health care when and where it is needed. We connect clinicians to populations that otherwise would face significant barriers to receiving mental health care. Flexibility: Array works with clinicians to develop schedules across an array of clinical settings that match their individual lifestyles and preferences. 

The pay range for this role is $63,000 - $93,500 per year. This information reflects the anticipated salary range for this position using current market data. The final offer will be determined based on an individual's skills, years of experience, education, capacity, licenses, and other job-related factors permitted by law.
